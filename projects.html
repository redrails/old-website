2015: EncryPaste

I created EncryPaste mainly as a thing that I could use to send my friends messages that I don't want others seeing, but I think it's a great tool to use in general. If you want to send something to someone without having to worry about your info being stored, encryption is the way to go and EncryPaste provides simple use of AES to share messages with privacy.
