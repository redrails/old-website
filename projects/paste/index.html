About Paste

I made a pastebin using PHP a while ago, and so I decided to create an Android application to make use of it. It's still under a lot of development and I plan on bringing a lot of features to it!

Development

Paste was originally made in PHP with very basic ideas, and is also open-source. This Android app is also open-source and you can customise it to your liking and use it with your own website! To build, you can edit: 
URLValues.java
with your own values, and then simply build the project! Please leave credits.
